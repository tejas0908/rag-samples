The institute has a very active NSS wing which organizes events which is very impactful to societal cause. regular Plantation of saplings, blood donation camps, vaccination drives during pandemic, visit to old age homes and distribution clothes, food packets, social awareness through walkathons, street plays, flashmobs.

RNS Management Alumni Association (MAA) has been actively participating in activities of social relevance since its inception. The major activities include food drives during pandemic, distribution of food items to orphan kids, stationery distribution for rural kids, donation of tri cycles to differently abled people and tree plantation. The association has a dream project of village adoption and is about to kickstart this year.
